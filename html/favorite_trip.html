Favorite Class Trip

In this article, I will tell you my favorite class trip.

HorseBack Riding

My favorite class trip is horseback riding. I have never ridden horse and gone to ranch. When I arrived at the ranch, a white cat and dog welcomed me.


After that, the owner introduced about his horses, and he told me I can ride horse. Iâ€™m not good at animal, so I was a little scared, but I encourage myself to ride it.


While riding a horse, ranch staff led a horse by the rein and asked me something. For example, this study abroad, what am I studying in university and what grade am I. After I get off the horse, I was so satisfied with it.

Activity

After that, I did three activities, pushing a handcart, walking with two boards, and seesaw game. When pushing the cart, we organize three-person team. Two blindfolded people push the cart, and one person rode on cart to tell visual information for two people. To tell and hear information was so difficult.

Next, walking with the board. About 10 people on the board held a rope and we go ahead with a signal. It was going well till halfway, but the ranch owner made hurdles. First, one person wore a blindfold. We got stacked. Second, one person turned around. We got stuck again.

Last activity is seesaw game. People around the seesaw tell some information to touch a cone placed on both side of it. The seesaw was big, so it was so exciting.

Conclusion

The class trip was so fun and very practical.

From those activities, I learned importance of challenge, teamwork, and tell information carefully yet briefly.
